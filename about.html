About

Hi, I'm Mark. Recently I have used my expertise in object oriented programming, PHP, Laravel 5 and database design to build an enterprise e-commerce web application. Customer experience was improved with a fast and responsive web store, new ways to pay and online management of accounts. Administration costs were reduced with significant time savings enabling staff to focus on other business needs.

I also used problem solving skills to devise an automated customer migration strategy to gradually move customers over to the new payment system resulting in IT staff support savings of 100+ hours.

Previously as a game programmer I have used expertise in C# and Mono (Xamarin) to design, implement and optimise algorithms to deliver mobile game content. This resulted in the release of two iOS social quiz games with over 1 million plays that enabled the company to break into the mobile market.

In the same role I have made use of ASP.NET, XML, XSLT, database design and version control to architect a text localisation pipeline for XBox360 Kinect Nat Geo TV. This resulted in content writers able to deliver text for the game.

My approach is always to conscientiously serve your needs so you attain your project goals and outcomes.

I've made programming my career because there always seems to be more to learn and the industry is always changing. I love talking with other coders and sharing experiences with technology.

I believe that great software is built on good communication and transparency.
