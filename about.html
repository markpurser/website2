About

I started as a freelancer in 2015, initially creating an e-commerce web application in the charity sector.

Since then I have been developing open source software, troubleshooting web sites and coding financial trading indicators.

My past experience has been in the aviation/flight simulation industry for 10 years. I have also 3 years experience in VR, 3 years console games and another 2 years in the medical sector. The latter included safety-critical software to IEC 61508 and IEC 62304.

I've made programming my career because there always seems to be more to learn and the industry is always changing. I love talking with other coders and sharing experiences with technology.

I believe that great software is built on good communication and transparency.
